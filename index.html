Training Wheels is a web platform designed for those who are just starting their driving journey and want to pass their exam!

A person can improve their knowledge with many tutorials, pop-up questions and even tests. The more a person progresses, the more rewards they will have to help them be on top of their game.

Alongside my team-mates, my role in the making of this project came in the designing, developing the front-end and making sense of the techniques to use inorder to incentivize the young-adults in their first step to learn how to drive, since i was in a similar situation at the time!

This project's objective is to present the user with a traffic situation and help them understand how the vehicles should proceed in a fun way!

Directed to a vr experience, the user is presented with a situation in which 3 different vehicles are in an intersecting road and the user must pick which must cross first according to the traffic laws.

This exercise was made with a-frame and javascript and my role was mainly coding all the traffic situations and how the vehicles behave in each, although i aided in picking the assets.
