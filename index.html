Evergreen is the name of lastest web platform me and my teamates have developed and it's meant to aid students to contribute in helping the environment while being apart of the project "Eco-Escolas".

This project was made with vue and it allows the user to participate in activities, register ocurrences, complete missions, collect points and badges and race to become the best contributor for a better planet!

Recently this year me and my team were nominated to participate in the 3rd edition of the Gamify it contest and, to our surprise, our design won!

Me and my teammates worked hard in every stage of the making of this project, from the conceptualization to the design, all the way to the development of the front-end and currently we're working on the back-end.

Training Wheels is a web platform designed for those who are just starting their driving journey and want to pass their exam!

A person can improve their knowledge with many tutorials, pop-up questions and even tests. The more a person progresses, the more rewards they will have to help them be on top of their game.

Alongside my team-mates, my role in the making of this project came in the designing, developing the front-end and making sense of the techniques to use inorder to incentivize the young-adults in their first step to learn how to drive, since i was in a similar situation at the time!

This project's objective is to present the user with a traffic situation and help them understand how the vehicles should proceed in a fun way!

Directed to a vr experience, the user is presented with a situation in which 3 different vehicles are in an intersecting road and the user must pick which must cross first according to the traffic laws.

This exercise was made with a-frame and javascript and my role was mainly coding all the traffic situations and how the vehicles behave in each, although i aided in picking the assets.

This game created with canvas was made with the purpose of teaching children a little bit about recycling.

The player is able to pick between three characters and even has the oportunity to name it! Once it's thrown into the game this character will give the player hints as to what to do throughout the different steps of the game.

With canvas and javascript me and my team were able to produce a very complete project, and my role in it was to map the game, code the characters mechanisms, implement the tree planting, hints, character picking and naming aspects, as well as implement sound effects and a conclusion for the game and include some of the css animations.

Check out our code

This mini game made with three.js was based off the game created previously, Eco-Meow, in its concept and design.

The purpose of this game was to build off the first one, but in a 3d setting. In this version of the game, the player experiences the first and last aspect of the previous, the character picking, being able to control all 3, and planting trees!

I was responsible for creating and maping the cenario and all it's objects, as well as it's animations, building the black cat character that is found in the middle, it's animations and interactions.
